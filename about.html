About Conference

Welcome to ICETAS 2023!

The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Applied Sciences (ICETAS) 2023 provides a platform for researchers, engineers, and academics from all around the world to present and discuss their latest findings and research results in various areas of applied sciences. The conference will take place from [insert date] and will be held online due to the ongoing global situation.

ICETAS 2023 aims to foster collaboration, exchange of ideas, and promote research activities in applied sciences, with the goal of making a positive impact on society. We invite researchers, students, and professionals to join us in this exciting event and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in various fields.

We look forward to your participation in ICETAS 2023!

The theme of this conference is “Engineering, Technologies & Application of Applied Sciences are Driving our Future”. 8th IEEE International Conference on Engineering Technologies and Applied Sciences (ICETAS) will provide a meeting place for the sharing of novel ideas and research findings in the field of engineering, technologies, Applied sciences.
