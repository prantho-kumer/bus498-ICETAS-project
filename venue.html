Venue

University of Technology Bahrain

Address: University of Technology Bahrain 1213 Block 712 Bldg 829, Salmabad, Bahrain.

The University of Technology Bahrain (UTB) (formerly known as AMA International University Bahrain) is a dynamic higher education institution located in Salmabad, Kingdom of Bahrain. Established in 2002 as AMA International University Bahrain, UTB has grown rapidly to become one of the leading universities in the region, offering undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields such as engineering, business, and computer science.

ICETAS 2023 will be held at the UTB campus. The university boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, labs, and libraries. The campus also features a variety of sports and recreational facilities, such as a gym, swimming pool, and football field, which are available to students and staff.

The university is conveniently located in Salmabad, which is easily accessible by public transportation and is only a 20-minute drive from the Bahrain International Airport. The area is known for its vibrant community and offers a variety of dining and shopping options for visitors.

We hope this information provides a glimpse into the UTB campus and the surrounding area. We look forward to welcoming you to ICETAS 2023 in person!

The theme of this conference is “Engineering, Technologies & Application of Applied Sciences are Driving our Future”. 8th IEEE International Conference on Engineering Technologies and Applied Sciences (ICETAS) will provide a meeting place for the sharing of novel ideas and research findings in the field of engineering, technologies, Applied sciences.
